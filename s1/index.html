S1-INBOX

The principal goals of the S1-INBOX were to deploy autonomous platforms such as floats to acquire physical-biogeochemical data that could be used to resolve mesoscale eddies, and to quantify impacts of physical processes on biogeochemical phenomena in the oligotrophic subtropical ocean.
